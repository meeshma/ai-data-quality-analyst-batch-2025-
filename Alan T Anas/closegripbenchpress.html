Close Grip Bench Press

Muscles Worked

  • Triceps
  • Chest (secondary)
  • Front Deltoids

How to Perform

  • Lie on a bench with hands shoulder-width apart on the bar.
  • Lower the bar to your lower chest while keeping elbows close.
  • Press the bar upward, focusing on triceps.

Tips

  • Keep elbows tucked in — don’t flare.
  • Use a smooth and controlled motion.
  • Do not grip too narrow (dangerous for wrists).

Common Mistakes

  • Going too narrow on the grip.
  • Letting elbows flare out.
  • Arching lower back excessively.
