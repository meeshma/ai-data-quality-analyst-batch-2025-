Incline Dumbbell Press

Muscles Worked

  • Upper Chest
  • Front Shoulders
  • Triceps

How to Perform

  • Set the bench to 30–45° incline.
  • Hold dumbbells at shoulder height.
  • Press upward and slightly inward.
  • Lower in a controlled motion.
  • Repeat for desired reps.

Tips

  • Keep elbows slightly tucked.
  • Do not over-arch your back.
  • Focus on the top half of the chest.

Common Mistakes

  • Bench angle too high.
  • Letting dumbbells drift outward.
  • Bouncing off the chest.
