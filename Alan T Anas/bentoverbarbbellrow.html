Bent Over Barbell Row

Muscles Worked

  • Lats
  • Rhomboids
  • Lower Back
  • Rear Delts
  • Biceps

How to Perform

  • Stand with feet shoulder width and grip the bar slightly wider than shoulders.
  • Bend at the hips until your torso is at a 45° angle.
  • Keep your back flat and core tight.
  • Pull the bar toward your lower chest or upper stomach.
  • Squeeze your back, then lower the bar in control.

Tips

  • Push hips back, not down.
  • Keep elbows close to your body.
  • Do not round your back.
  • Use controlled movements, avoid jerking.

Common Mistakes

  • Rounding the lower back.
  • Standing too upright.
  • Using momentum instead of muscle contraction.
  • Bar drifting too far from the body.
