Plank

Muscles Worked

  • Core (Rectus Abdominis)
  • Obliques
  • Transverse Abdominis
  • Lower Back
  • Shoulders
  • Glutes

How to Perform

  • Start on the floor with your forearms on the ground and elbows under your shoulders.
  • Extend your legs straight behind you, resting on your toes.
  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Engage your core and glutes to maintain a rigid position.
  • Hold the position for the desired amount of time.

Tips

  • Keep your hips level—don’t let them rise or sag.
  • Look at the floor to keep your neck neutral.
  • Breathe steadily throughout the hold.
  • Squeeze your glutes to increase stability.

Common Mistakes

  • Letting hips drop or arch upward.
  • Holding breath during the exercise.
  • Placing elbows too far forward.
  • Swaying or moving the body too much.
