Calf Raises

Muscles Worked

  • Gastrocnemius
  • Soleus
  • Ankle Stabilizers

How to Perform

  • Stand upright on a flat surface or step.
  • Raise heels as high as possible by extending ankles.
  • Squeeze calves at the top.
  • Lower slowly back down.
  • Repeat for desired reps.

Tips

  • Move in full range of motion.
  • Pause at the top for a stronger contraction.
  • Perform slowly — avoid bouncing.
  • Hold onto something for balance if needed.

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing the movement.
  • Not lowering heels fully.
  • Using momentum instead of control.
  • Knees bending too much.
