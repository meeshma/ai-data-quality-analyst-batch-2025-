Barbell Bench Press

Muscles Worked

  • Chest (Pectoralis Major)
  • Front Shoulders (Anterior Deltoids)
  • Triceps
  • Stabilizers: Lats & Upper Back

How to Perform

  • Lie flat on the bench with your feet firmly planted.
  • Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  • Unrack the bar and hold it above your chest with straight arms.
  • Lower the bar slowly to mid-chest level.
  • Press the bar upward while keeping your elbows slightly tucked.
  • Lock out at the top and repeat.

Tips for Better Form

  • Keep your back slightly arched and shoulders pulled back.
  • Do not bounce the bar off your chest—control the movement.
  • Maintain a stable foot position throughout the lift.
  • Use a spotter when lifting heavy.

Common Mistakes

  • Flaring elbows too wide.
  • Bench pressing with unstable feet.
  • Lowering the bar too high (towards the neck).
  • Lifting your hips off the bench.
