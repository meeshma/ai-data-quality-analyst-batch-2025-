Dumbbell Curl

Muscles Worked

  • Biceps Brachii
  • Brachialis
  • Forearms

How to Perform

  • Stand with your torso upright and dumbbells at your sides.
  • Keep elbows close to your torso.
  • Curl the dumbbells upward while squeezing your biceps.
  • Lower them slowly to the starting position.

Tips

  • Do not swing the weights.
  • Keep wrists straight — don’t bend them.
  • Squeeze biceps at the top.

Common Mistakes

  • Using momentum to lift the weight.
  • Letting elbows drift forward.
  • Not lowering through full range of motion.
