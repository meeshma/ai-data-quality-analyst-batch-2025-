🏋️‍♂️ Build Muscle

Increase strength and muscle size using progressive overload and recovery-based programming.

Training Plan

  • Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
  • Pull Day (Back & Biceps)
  • Leg Day (Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings)
  • Upper/Lower Strength Split

Muscle-Building Tips

  • Increase weight gradually (progressive overload)
  • Eat more protein (1.6g–2.2g per kg bodyweight)
  • Rest 48 hours between heavy sessions
