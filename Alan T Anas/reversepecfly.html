Reverse Pec Fly

Muscles Worked

  • Rear Deltoids
  • Rhomboids
  • Trapezius
  • Rotator Cuff Stabilizers

How to Perform

  • Sit on the pec deck machine facing the pads.
  • Grab the handles with arms extended slightly.
  • Pull arms backward in a reverse fly motion.
  • Squeeze rear delts at the end of the movement.
  • Return slowly to the start.

Tips

  • Keep chest pressed firmly against the pad.
  • Use smooth, controlled movement.
  • Focus on squeezing rear delts, not traps.
  • Adjust seat height so arms are parallel.

Common Mistakes

  • Using too much weight.
  • Shrugging shoulders during the movement.
  • Leaning back excessively.
  • Letting weights slam back.
