Hammer Curl

Muscles Worked

  • Brachialis
  • Brachioradialis
  • Biceps

How to Perform

  • Hold dumbbells with a neutral (hammer) grip.
  • Keep elbows tucked close to your body.
  • Curl up while maintaining the hammer grip.
  • Lower slowly to the starting position.

Tips

  • Keep wrists straight.
  • Squeeze the top position.
  • Move weights with control.

Common Mistakes

  • Swinging the dumbbells.
  • Letting elbows flare out.
  • Going too fast on the negative.
