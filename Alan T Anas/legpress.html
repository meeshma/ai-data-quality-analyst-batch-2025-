Leg Press

Muscles Worked

  • Quadriceps
  • Glutes
  • Hamstrings
  • Calves

How to Perform

  • Sit on the leg press machine with your back flat against the pad.
  • Place feet shoulder-width apart on the platform.
  • Release the safety handles and extend legs without locking knees.
  • Lower the platform until knees reach a 90° angle.
  • Push back up through heels to the starting position.

Tips

  • Keep your lower back flat against the pad.
  • Avoid locking your knees at the top.
  • Push through heels, not toes.
  • Control the movement—no bouncing.

Common Mistakes

  • Letting knees cave inward.
  • Placing feet too low (increases knee strain).
  • Overloading weight.
  • Locking knees at full extension.
