Bicycle Crunches

Muscles Worked

  • Rectus Abdominis (Upper & Lower Abs)
  • Obliques (Primary)
  • Hip Flexors

How to Perform

  • Lie on your back with your hands behind your head.
  • Lift your shoulders off the floor and raise your legs.
  • Bring your right elbow toward your left knee while straightening your right leg.
  • Switch sides — bring your left elbow toward your right knee while extending the left leg.
  • Continue alternating in a pedaling motion.

Tips

  • Twist from your core, not your elbows.
  • Keep your lower back pressed into the floor.
  • Move slowly and with control for better muscle activation.
  • Fully extend each leg during the movement.

Common Mistakes

  • Pulling on the neck with your hands.
  • Using momentum instead of core rotation.
  • Short, quick movements with no full extension.
  • Raising the lower back off the floor.
