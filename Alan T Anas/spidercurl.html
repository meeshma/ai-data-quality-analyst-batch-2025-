Spider Curl

Muscles Worked

  • Biceps Brachii (peak emphasis)
  • Brachialis

How to Perform

  • Lay chest-down on an incline bench.
  • Let arms hang straight down holding dumbbells.
  • Curl upward without swinging.
  • Squeeze biceps at the top and lower slowly.

Tips

  • Keep your chest on the bench.
  • Use lighter weights for strict form.
  • Focus on slow eccentrics.

Common Mistakes

  • Using momentum.
  • Letting elbows move backward.
  • Arching off the bench.
