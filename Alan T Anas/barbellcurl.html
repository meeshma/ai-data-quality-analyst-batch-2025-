Barbell Curls

Muscles Worked

  • Biceps Brachii
  • Brachialis
  • Forearms

How to Perform

  • Stand tall with feet shoulder-width apart and grip the barbell with an underhand grip.
  • Keep elbows close to your torso and curl the bar upward.
  • Contract your biceps at the top while avoiding swinging.
  • Lower the bar slowly under control to the starting position.

Tips

  • Keep your upper arms locked to avoid using momentum.
  • Squeeze your biceps at the top for maximum activation.
  • Use a weight that allows strict form.
  • Do not arch your back while lifting.

Common Mistakes

  • Swinging the body to lift heavier weights.
  • Letting elbows flare out too much.
  • Not fully extending arms at the bottom.
  • Using wrists excessively instead of biceps.
