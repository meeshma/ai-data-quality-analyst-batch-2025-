Romanian Deadlift

Muscles Worked

  • Hamstrings
  • Glutes
  • Lower Back
  • Core

How to Perform

  • Stand with feet hip-width apart holding a barbell.
  • Keep back straight and hinge at the hips.
  • Lower the bar down along your legs until hamstrings stretch.
  • Keep knees slightly bent throughout.
  • Drive hips forward to return to standing.

Tips

  • Keep the bar close to your legs.
  • Hinge at hips—don’t squat down.
  • Maintain a flat back.
  • Move slowly and control the descent.

Common Mistakes

  • Rounding the back.
  • Bending knees too much.
  • Letting the bar drift forward.
  • Using too much weight early on.
