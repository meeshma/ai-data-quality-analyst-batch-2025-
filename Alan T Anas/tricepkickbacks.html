Tricep Kickback

Muscles Worked

  • Triceps (long head focus)
  • Shoulder stabilizers

How to Perform

  • Bend forward while holding a dumbbell.
  • Keep your elbow fixed close to your body.
  • Extend your arm backward until fully straight.
  • Return slowly to the starting position.

Tips

  • Use light weight for strict form.
  • Avoid swinging your arm.
  • Keep your upper arm parallel to the floor.

Common Mistakes

  • Dropping the elbow too low.
  • Using weight that's too heavy.
  • Not reaching full extension.
