Tricep Pushdown

Muscles Worked

  • Triceps (all heads)
  • Forearms (stabilization)

How to Perform

  • Stand facing a cable machine with the rope or bar attached.
  • Keep elbows tightly tucked at your sides.
  • Push the handle downward until arms fully extend.
  • Slowly return to the starting position.

Tips

  • Do not let your elbows flare out.
  • Use only your triceps — avoid swinging.
  • Slightly lean forward for a better contraction.

Common Mistakes

  • Letting elbows move forward.
  • Using momentum to push the weight.
  • Not locking out elbows properly.
