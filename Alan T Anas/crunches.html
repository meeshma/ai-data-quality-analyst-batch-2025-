Crunches

Muscles Worked

  • Rectus Abdominis (Upper Abs)
  • Obliques (Stabilization)
  • Hip Flexors (Minor involvement)

How to Perform

  • Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  • Place your hands behind your head or across your chest.
  • Lift your shoulders off the floor by contracting your abs.
  • Pause briefly at the top while squeezing the core.
  • Lower yourself down slowly with control.

Tips

  • Focus on lifting with your abs, not your neck.
  • Keep lower back pressed lightly against the floor.
  • Don’t pull your head forward with your hands.
  • Slow, controlled reps give better results.

Common Mistakes

  • Using momentum instead of core engagement.
  • Pulling on the neck.
  • Lifting too high — this turns it into a sit-up.
  • Rushing through repetitions.
