Preacher Curl

Muscles Worked

  • Biceps (peak contraction)
  • Brachialis

How to Perform

  • Sit on a preacher bench with your upper arms locked in place.
  • Hold the bar or dumbbells with an underhand grip.
  • Curl upward while keeping arms glued to the pad.
  • Lower slowly until arms are nearly straight.

Tips

  • Do not let elbows lift off the pad.
  • Use a full stretch at the bottom.
  • Keep movement slow and controlled.

Common Mistakes

  • Lifting elbows off the pad.
  • Using too much weight and swinging.
  • Shortening the range of motion.
