Single-Arm Dumbbell Row

Muscles Worked

  • Lats
  • Middle Back
  • Rhomboids
  • Rear Delts
  • Biceps

How to Perform

  • Place one knee and hand on a bench for support.
  • Hold a dumbbell in the opposite hand, arm extended down.
  • Pull the dumbbell toward your hip, keeping your elbow close.
  • Squeeze your back muscles at the top.
  • Lower with control and repeat.
  • Switch sides after completing the set.

Tips

  • Keep your torso square—don’t rotate.
  • Pull with your back, not your biceps.
  • Use a full range of motion.
  • Keep your core tight for stability.

Common Mistakes

  • Twisting your body during the pull.
  • Using momentum instead of muscle control.
  • Letting the dumbbell drop too fast.
  • Pulling straight up instead of toward the hip.
