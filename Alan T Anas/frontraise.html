Front Raises

Muscles Worked

  • Anterior Deltoids
  • Upper Chest
  • Serratus Anterior

How to Perform

  • Stand with dumbbells or a plate in front of your thighs.
  • Lift the weight straight up in front of you to shoulder height.
  • Pause briefly, then lower back down slowly.

Tips

  • Lift with shoulders, not wrists.
  • Keep torso upright — avoid swinging.
  • Use slow and controlled reps.
  • Stop at shoulder level — not above.

Common Mistakes

  • Using too heavy weights.
  • Leaning back to lift.
  • Overextending arms upward.
  • Jerking or swinging the weight.
