Lunges

Muscles Worked

  • Quadriceps
  • Glutes
  • Hamstrings
  • Calves
  • Core

How to Perform

  • Stand upright with feet hip-width apart.
  • Step forward with one leg and lower hips until both knees are 90°.
  • Keep torso upright and core braced.
  • Push through the front heel to return to standing.
  • Alternate legs for each rep.

Tips

  • Keep front knee aligned with toes.
  • Maintain a tall posture.
  • Step far enough to create a stable base.
  • Avoid leaning forward.

Common Mistakes

  • Knee caving inward.
  • Heel lifting off the ground.
  • Leaning forward excessively.
  • Short step length reducing stability.
