Overhead Press

Muscles Worked

  • Anterior Deltoids
  • Lateral Deltoids
  • Triceps
  • Upper Chest
  • Trapezius
  • Core and Stabilizers

How to Perform

  • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and grip the bar just outside shoulder width.
  • Hold the bar at upper chest level with elbows slightly forward.
  • Brace your core and press the bar straight overhead.
  • Fully extend your arms without arching your lower back.
  • Lower the bar back down under control to the starting position.

Tips

  • Keep your core tight to prevent your back from hyperextending.
  • Squeeze your glutes to stabilize your body.
  • Press the bar in a straight line for maximum stability.
  • Avoid flaring your elbows too wide.

Common Mistakes

  • Leaning back excessively (risking lower back injury).
  • Pressing the bar forward instead of upward.
  • Using legs too much — turning it into a push press unintentionally.
  • Not locking out arms at the top.
