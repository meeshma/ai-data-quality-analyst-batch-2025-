Seated Cable Row

Muscles Worked

  • Middle Back
  • Lats
  • Rhomboids
  • Biceps
  • Rear Delts

How to Perform

  • Sit on the bench and place your feet firmly on the platform.
  • Grab the handle with both hands.
  • Keep your chest up and torso upright.
  • Pull the handle toward your waist while squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  • Slowly extend back to the starting position.

Tips

  • Keep your back straight, avoid rounding.
  • Lead the movement with your elbows.
  • Keep your shoulders down and relaxed.
  • Use a controlled motion, avoid leaning back excessively.

Common Mistakes

  • Using momentum by swinging the torso.
  • Letting shoulders shrug upward.
  • Pushing legs or leaning too far back.
  • Not fully stretching the lats at the start position.
