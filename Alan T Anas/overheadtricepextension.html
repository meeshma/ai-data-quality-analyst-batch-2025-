Overhead Tricep Extension

Muscles Worked

  • Triceps (long head focus)
  • Core stabilizers

How to Perform

  • Hold a dumbbell with both hands overhead.
  • Keep your elbows pointing forward.
  • Lower the dumbbell behind your head.
  • Extend your arms fully to return to starting position.

Tips

  • Keep elbows close — do not flare out.
  • Move slowly through full range of motion.
  • Engage core to avoid arching your back.

Common Mistakes

  • Flaring elbows outward.
  • Using excessive weight.
  • Arching the lower back too much.
