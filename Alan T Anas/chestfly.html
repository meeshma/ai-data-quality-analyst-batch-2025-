Chest Fly

Muscles Worked

  • Inner Chest
  • Outer Chest
  • Front Shoulders

How to Perform

  • Keep elbows slightly bent.
  • Bring handles together in a wide arc.
  • Squeeze chest at the center.
  • Return slowly to starting position.

Tips

  • Avoid bending elbows too much.
  • Focus on stretch and squeeze.
  • Use lightweight for control.

Common Mistakes

  • Using too much weight.
  • Turning it into a press.
  • Not staying in the same plane of motion.
