Push-Ups

Muscles Worked

  • Chest
  • Shoulders
  • Triceps
  • Core

How to Perform

  • Place hands slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  • Keep body straight from head to heels.
  • Lower chest to just above the ground.
  • Push back up while exhaling.

Tips

  • Do not drop hips.
  • Keep elbows tucked at 45°.
  • Engage your core.

Common Mistakes

  • Sagging or raising hips.
  • Too wide hand placement.
  • Partial reps.
