Chest Dips

Muscles Worked

  • Lower Chest
  • Triceps
  • Front Shoulders

How to Perform

  • Lean your torso forward.
  • Lower yourself until elbows reach 90°.
  • Push upward focusing on chest contraction.

Tips

  • Keep legs behind you.
  • Lean more forward for chest activation.
  • Use controlled movement.

Common Mistakes

  • Staying too upright (turns it into triceps dips).
  • Dropping too low.
  • Not leaning forward.
