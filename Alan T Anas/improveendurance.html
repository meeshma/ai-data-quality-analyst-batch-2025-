⚡ Improve Endurance

Boost cardiovascular performance, stamina, and total-body energy with structured endurance training.

Endurance Training

  • Steady-State Cardio (45–60 min)
  • Interval Sprints (HIIT)
  • Long-distance running or cycling
  • Rowing & swimming sessions

Performance Tips

  • Increase weekly distance gradually (5–10%)
  • Hydrate + electrolytes during long sessions
  • Practice breath control and pacing
