About Us

Venzo is a platform designed to bring people together through memorable events, celebrations, and experiences. We create moments that connect communities and inspire creativity.

Our platform allows users to discover, track, and engage with events as they happen. By emphasizing real-time updates and live information, Venzo ensures that audiences never miss what’s happening around them.

At Venzo, we believe the power of events lies in the present moment. That’s why everything we build is centered around real-time connection, immediacy, and shared experiences that bring people together — right now.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Venzo about?
Venzo is a platform that connects people through real-time events, experiences, and celebrations.
How do I register for an event?
You can register by clicking on the "View Programs" button under any event category and following the registration steps.
Can I host my own event on Venzo?
Yes! Venzo allows organizers to host events. Contact us for more information.
Are tickets refundable?
Tickets are non-refundable unless the date of the event changes or is cancelled due to Government restrictions.
Do I need to bring ID?
Almost all venues we run events at operate a strict No ID No Entry Policy. So we recommend everyone brings Photo ID.
